Chapter 00 — Origins

About me.

"I believe stories are the threads that weave people together."

Whether through words on a page, voices in a podcast, or ideas shared in a community project, I find myself chasing the magic of connection. My name is My My, and this is where I collect the fragments of my journey — a blend of literature, media, and the simple wish to make an impact through storytelling.

Over the past three years that's meant writing short stories and essays, building a podcast from zero listeners, leading community health and wellbeing projects, and pitching original business ideas in national case competitions — always circling back to the same question: how do you make someone feel truly heard?
